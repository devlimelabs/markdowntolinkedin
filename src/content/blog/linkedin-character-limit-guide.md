## Why Character Limits Matter

Understanding character limits helps you:
- **Avoid truncated posts** that cut off mid-sentence
- **Optimize the preview** that people see in their feed
- **Plan content length** appropriately
- **Maximize engagement** by using space wisely

## The Critical "Fold" Lines

Not all characters are equal. Here's what shows before "see more":

**Desktop feed:** ~210 characters
**Mobile feed:** ~150 characters
**Post page:** Full content

> Your hook needs to work within ~150 characters to capture mobile users.

## Maximizing Your Space

### Do's

1. **Front-load value** - Put the best content first
2. **Use line breaks** - They don't count toward the limit
3. **Be concise** - Every word should earn its place
4. **Format wisely** - Bold text takes same space as regular

### Don'ts

1. **Don't pad with emojis** - One or two max
2. **Don't repeat yourself** - Say it once, say it well
3. **Don't add unnecessary disclaimers** - Get to the point
4. **Don't stuff hashtags** - 3-5 relevant ones maximum

## Common Questions

**Q: What happens if I exceed the limit?**
A: LinkedIn simply won't let you post. You'll need to trim your content.

**Q: Do hashtags count toward the limit?**
A: Yes, hashtags count as regular characters including the # symbol.

**Q: Do URLs count at full length?**
A: Yes, LinkedIn doesn't shorten URLs in the character count.
