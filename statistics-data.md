# LinkedIn Statistics for Blog Post

## Platform Usage (2025)
- LinkedIn has 1.2 billion members
- 1.77 billion monthly visits in February 2025
- Users spend average of 48 hours per month on mobile
- Most popular with users aged 25-34
- 57% of LinkedIn traffic comes from mobile devices
- 90% of leads convert on desktop (despite mobile traffic)

## Engagement Statistics
- 25% of users interact with brand content daily
- Users most likely to engage with text posts
- Median engagement rate: 8.01% (January 2025), up from 6.00% (January 2024)
- Best posting times: Tuesdays-Thursdays, 10 AM - 12 PM
- LinkedIn carousels get 30% more weekly views

## B2B Marketing
- 89% of B2B marketers use LinkedIn for lead generation
- 97% of B2B marketers use LinkedIn as part of content marketing strategy
- LinkedIn ads show 33% increase in purchase intent
- 57% of marketers consider LinkedIn effective for marketing

## Video Content
- Videos under 15 seconds get most engagement
- Video gets the most shares on LinkedIn
- Users creating video at 2x rate of other content formats

## Mobile vs Desktop Key Insight
- C-Suite executives: 96% mobile usage
- Legal professionals: 95% mobile usage
- But 80-90% of conversions still happen on desktop
- Mobile dominates impressions, desktop dominates conversions

## Formatting Relevance
- Good formatting makes people stop scrolling
- 1 billion+ members means more competition for attention
- Visual tweaks can make posts 10x easier to follow
- Well-structured posts more likely to be read through
